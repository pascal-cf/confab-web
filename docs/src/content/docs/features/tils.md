title: TILs
description: Today-I-Learned snippets surfaced from your sessions.
---

A **TIL** is a short, self-contained piece of knowledge — a command, a pattern, a gotcha, a fix — extracted from your sessions and surfaced as a browsable feed.

## How they're generated

TILs are extracted at upload time via an LLM pass over the transcript. Each TIL links back to the originating session and message, so you can dive into the full context whenever you need.

## Deep-linking

TIL cards on the dashboard deep-link into the originating row in the transcript viewer, scrolling and highlighting on arrival.

## Filtering

The TIL feed shares the same filter UI as Sessions — provider, repo, owner, date range, and free-text search.
